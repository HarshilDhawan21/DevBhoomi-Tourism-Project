12 Jyotirlingas
The sacred abodes of Lord Shiva — luminous pillars of divine light

Somnath

Prabhas Patan, Gujarat — The first Jyotirlinga.

Mallikarjuna

Srisailam, Andhra Pradesh — Nestled in the Nallamala hills.

Mahakaleshwar

Ujjain, Madhya Pradesh — Famous for its Bhasma Aarti.

Omkareshwar

Khandwa, Madhya Pradesh — On the sacred island shaped like ‘Om’.

Kedarnath

Uttarakhand — Amidst the Himalayas, part of Char Dham.

Baidyanath

Deoghar, Jharkhand — Associated with Ravana’s devotion.

Nageshwar

Dwarka, Gujarat — Protector from all poisons.

Trimbakeshwar

Nashik, Maharashtra — Source of the holy Godavari river.

Kashi Vishwanath

Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh — The spiritual capital of India.

Vaidyanath (Parli)

Parli, Maharashtra — Known as the healer Jyotirlinga.

Rameshwaram

Tamil Nadu — Closely linked with Lord Rama’s journey.

Grishneshwar

Ellora, Maharashtra — Near the famous Ellora caves.
